# About Me

Hi, I’m **Noureldin**, a passionate **DevOps Engineer** with over 2 years of experience in building and managing cloud infrastructure, optimizing CI/CD pipelines, and enhancing system performance and security. I specialize in creating scalable, efficient, and secure systems that empower teams to deliver high-quality software faster.

### What I Do
I’m proficient in **GCP, Terraform, Golang, Docker, Kubernetes, and Bash scripting**, and I have a strong background in **Infrastructure as Code (IaC)** and **cloud-native solutions**. My work focuses on streamlining deployment processes, improving developer productivity, and implementing observability solutions like distributed tracing to ensure system reliability.

Some of my key achievements include:
- Developing internal tools like **Deployer** (a Golang-based deployment automation tool) and **Cloudenv** (for environment management), which significantly improved deployment efficiency.
- Reducing CI/CD pipeline runtime by **30%** through strategic optimizations.
- Implementing **distributed tracing** in Django applications using OpenTelemetry to enhance observability and troubleshooting.
- Embedding **security into the development lifecycle** using DevSecOps practices and tools like Defect Dojo.

### My Journey
I hold a **Bachelor’s degree in Communication and Electronics Engineering**, where I graduated with a focus on implementing DevOps and cloud technologies for hosting software products. My passion for technology led me to certifications like **Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA)**, **AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner**, and **Microsoft Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900)**.

Beyond my professional work, I’ve volunteered as a **Cybersecurity Member** and **Social Media Member** at “CAT Reloaded,” a tech community at Mansoura University, where I contributed to promoting cybersecurity awareness and tech innovation.

### Why I Love DevOps
I’m fascinated by the intersection of development and operations, where technology meets efficiency. DevOps isn’t just a job for me—it’s a mindset. I thrive on solving complex problems, automating processes, and building systems that scale seamlessly while maintaining security and reliability.

I’m always open to discussing new opportunities, collaborations, or just chatting about technology. Feel free to reach out to me via links below.
